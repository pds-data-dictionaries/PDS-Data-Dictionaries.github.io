LDD Users Groups
========================

Applicable only to the PDS Common and Discipline Local Data Dictionaries, each dictionary has a user's group mailing list to participate and collaborate on future designs and implementations, and receive notifications of new releases of the dictionaries.


Membership
+++++++++++

Anyone from the PDS user community can become a member of a Users Group. Send an email to the applicable Discipline Dictionary Steward (link TBD) to gain access to group communications.
